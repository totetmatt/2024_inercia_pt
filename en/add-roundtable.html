Roundtable on the topic of empowering for the digital arts, with a reflection on it's importance and the challenges at a local level. Invited participants:

  • Gonçalo Negrão, dean and professor of Escola Superior de Tecnologia e Gestão Jean Piaget in Almada.
  • Artur Coelho, ICT teacher and PTD coordinator at AE Venda do Pinheiro, teacher trainer (ANPRI/CFAERC/ASSP), EU Codeweek Leading Teacher (PT), member of Lab Aberto Fablab.
  • Paulo Lopes, representative of Sharkcoders Seixal and Sharkcoders Almada.
  • Daniel Índias Fernandes, artist and educator at TUMO Coimbra dedicated to exploring the integration of creativity and technology through a biocentric lens, tackling the challenges of the modern world with mindful and regenerative solutions.
  • Hugo Pinto representative member of the Load ZX Spectrum museum.
  • Nuno Carvalho, representing Rato - ADCC, a youth organization from the region of Setúbal that develops projects of social inclusion in the field of Information and Communication Technologies.
  • João Vargues, President of the Executive Comission of Museu Zer0, a digital arte museum in Santa Catarina da Fonte do Bispo.
