Inércia Trophies

Inércia Demoparty trophies are exclusive for the winners of Inércia demoscene competitions. Money can't buy this, you'll need to convince the audience you are worthy of receiving one!

Designed and fabricated by Jae686.

Party T-Shirts

Do you like our logo? You're going to love our t-shirts!

We also have some sizes available with the design from the 2023 edition.

Electronic Badge

Missed last year’s Inércia 2023 electronic badge? This year, they’re back and even better! Inércia 2024 badges now come with a mini OLED screen—perfect for displaying custom text, animations, and more, alongside the ability to play your favorite .mod tunes.

This year, we’re thrilled to increase production to 50 badges, so more of you can get your hands on one. Each badge now comes pre-assembled, making it easier for us to produce more than last year. Badges are available on a first-come, first-served basis, with a limit of one badge per person to ensure as many people as possible can enjoy one. Don’t miss your chance to claim yours!
